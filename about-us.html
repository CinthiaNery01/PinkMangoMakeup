About Us

Join us in celebrating the magic of makeup that's as playful and daring as you are!

Sustentability

At Pink Mango, we believe beauty should empower without compromising the planet or its people. We're passionate about creating makeup that's as sustainable as it is vibrant, sourcing every ingredient ethically and ensuring that each product reflects our commitment to a better future. Our journey begins with mindful sourcing—partnering only with suppliers who uphold ethical practices and fair labor standards. We prioritize sustainable materials, minimize waste at every step, and actively seek out renewable packaging options. Because to us, being beautiful means being kind to the world we share. With Pink Mango, you can be confident that each product is crafted with love for you and respect for the planet. Together, we're redefining beauty—one mindful choice at a time.
